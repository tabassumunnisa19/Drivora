Our Story

We Started with a Simple Idea

DriveEase was born in 2021 in Kollam, Kerala, from a simple frustration — people owned good cars but couldn't always drive them. Medical conditions, fatigue, celebrations, or just the need for a trusted companion behind the wheel.

Our founder, a native of Kollam, saw families struggling to find reliable drivers for hospital trips and elderly relatives who needed assistance. He brought together a network of verified, professional drivers to solve this problem.

Today, DriveEase has grown into Kerala's most trusted personal driver service, with over 500 happy trips and an average rating of 4.9 stars. We're not just a booking platform — we're your partner on every journey.
